How do I exit or close the terminals?

Type exit to close the working terminal where you perform the CVS commands. To close the (minimized) terminal where you established your tunnel, perform a ^C at its command prompt. ^C is a combined keystroke called control-c. Press down the Cntl and c keys simutaneously to close the tunnel. The terminal responds with the following blurb.

^CConnection to openoffice.org closed.
