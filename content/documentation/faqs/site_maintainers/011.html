My public key has been uploaded to the server; what is the next step?

You know this has happened when they tell you your key has been uploaded. This will occur as a response from the support team on the issue where you sent your public key. Now you need the procedure to establish a tunnel: ( Linux | Mac | Windows ). You also need to request for permission to the project you wish to access. Do this on the same issue as where you submitted your public key.
