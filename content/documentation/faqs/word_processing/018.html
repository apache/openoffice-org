I have some text that I want to appear with the first character of each word in uppercase and the rest of the word in lowercase (initial uppercase). How do I do this automatically?

Follow these steps:

  • Select the desired text (by drag selecting)

  • From the pull down menus, select: Format -> Character

  • Click on the tab called 'Font Effects'

  • In the 'More Effects' area, click on the box next to 'Title Case'

  • Click on OK

This method also works for anything that can be drag-selected, such as a table row or column.
