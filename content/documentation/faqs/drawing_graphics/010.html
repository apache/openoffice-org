How can I redefine the origin of the drawing coordinates?

Do it in this way:

  1. Click on the intersection corner between the vertical and horizontal ruler and hold the mouse button down.
  2. When dragging the mouse you can see a hatched cross.
  3. Reach the point you want to use as your new origin of the drawing coordinates, then release the mouse button.
