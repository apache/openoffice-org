You are about to download Apache OpenOffice...

Please contribute!

While starting the download, we would like to remind you of some opportunities for contribution. Apache OpenOffice is provided to you at no charge, and you have every right to redistribute the software. We depend on people willing to contribute their time and support to the project to ensure that AOO remains free and available at no cost.

You can help

The basic options consist of: Contributing Work, Joining Mailing Lists, Reporting Bugs & Issues, Spreading the Word about Apache OpenOffice and finally Donating Funds.
(Note: Links will open in a new window)

Stay in touch

You may want to hear about announcements, release notifications, ... and receive our monthly newsletter. Please send a message to announce-subscribe@openoffice.apache.org to be added as subscriber to our announce list. The archive and more information are available on the lists overview page.

Any problems?

If you have problems with the download you may want to check the consistency of the file (MD5 checksum) or contact us. The Installation Instructions will help you with the first steps.
Professional and (free) volunteer support for Apache OpenOffice options are listed on our Support webpage.

We appreciate your support for the project.

If the download doesn't work for you, please manually select a mirror close to you. You'll find the Apache OpenOffice downloads in the subdirectory stable/version.
