Bangla/Bengali OpenOffice.org

Ankur group is the official team for Bangla OpenOffice.org. Translation work has already started, and our goal is to have Bangla as a supported language. OpenOffice.org 2.0 is already out with most of the menu entries being translated in Bangla. Future versions of OpenOffice.org will have more and more modules translated to Bangla.
