Terminating the tunnel

The easiest way to terminate the tunnel is to Ctrl-C (^C) it out of existence. In both the Mac OS 9.x and Windows environment, you can also close the client window, thereby shutting the tunnel down. Of course, in Mac OS X, the terminal window is closed using control-C.
