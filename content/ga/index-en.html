Welcome to the OpenOffice.org Irish localisation project

We're a small group of volunteers committed to providing free software in the Irish language. If you would like to help with translating, proofreading, testing, or other aspects of the project, please write to us at dev@ga.openoffice.org.

OpenOffice.org is an open source and open standards complete productivity suite compatible with all major office suites. It's available for free and language packs for version 3.2 are available in 67 languages.

So what does the suite include?

  • Writer: a powerful tool for creating professional documents, reports, newsl etters, and brochures
  • Calc: a feature-packed spreadsheet
  • Impress: the fastest, most powerful way to create effective multimedia pres entations
  • Draw: will produce everything from simple diagrams to dynamic 3D illustrati ons and special effects
  • Base: the tools you need for day to day database work in a simple spreadshe et-like form
  • Math: a component for mathematical equations
