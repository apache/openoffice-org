Performance Project

Charter

We are the performance team and our main goal is to improve the OpenOffice.org performance by identifying and solving performance bottlenecks that impair our users' productivity.

We analyze the status of OpenOffice.org performance and identify areas where the performance is not sufficient. Members of the project will improve the performance in these areas, and setup a system to monitor performance continuously.

We implement tools and tests to analyze the performance of individual OpenOffice.org master and child workspace (CWS) builds in order to detect and prevent performance declines and regressions.

Close cooperation with other projects in the OpenOffice.org community is the basis for the success of this cross-sectional activity.
