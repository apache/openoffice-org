Spotlight: On Developers and Technology


2001 November 19

User List

Do you have questions about using OpenOffice.org? Consider posing them to our excellent "users" list. The list, moderated by Community Manager Max Lanfranconi, and populated by a large number of experienced OpenOffice.org users, works in the classic Open Source manner: you have a question and someone on the list has an answer they want to share with you. Of course, the answers come at no charge to you, and they come fast. But most important, they contribute to building a community: the next time some one has a question, it might be you who volunteers to share your knowledge and answer that question. You can post without being subscribed; but to avoid the delay imposed by moderation, please go to our Mail List page and subscribe.
