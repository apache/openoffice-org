Welcome to the OpenOffice.org Nepali Native-lang project, which is part of The Native Language Confederation.

Madan Puraskar Pustakalaya is the official team for Nepali OpenOffice.org. More than 90% of the translation work for both GUI and Help files have been completed. OpenOffice.org 2.1 is already out with the inclusion of Nepali translations.
