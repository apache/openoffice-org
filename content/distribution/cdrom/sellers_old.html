CD-ROMs for consumers

This page allows you to find a distributor near you, or who will ship to your location.

There is also a standard CD image available for you to burn onto your own CD-R. Since this is a very large download (approximately 700 MB at this time), we also make it available through the BitTorrent Peer to Peer network. for information on BitTorrent, see the Peer To Peer project page .

Would you like to Be a Distributor too?

We welcome anyone who would like to distribute OpenOffice.org in their local community. See the Information for Distributors.
