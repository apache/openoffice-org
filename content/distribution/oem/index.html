OEM/Bundling of Apache OpenOffice

Apache OpenOffice is distributed under the Apache License 2.0, a permissive open source license. Section 4 "Redistribution" of that license describes the permissions and conditions related to redistribution of OpenOffice.

In general we encourage redistribution of OpenOffice and offer the following avenues for answering OEM questions:

  • For technical questions, related to customization of OpenOffice, pre-installs, extensions, rebranding, etc., we recommend posting to our public development mailing list.
  • For questions related to Apache License and its permissions, export controls, etc., you can consult the resources on the Apache Legal Affairs home page.
