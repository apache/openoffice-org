Articles and Interviews

$Date: 2007/05/11 14:12:40 $

Since the beginning of the OpenOffice.org project, we have periodically published articles and interviews. If you would like to submit an article, please contact me, Louis Suárez-Potts, editor and principal writer. Besides these articles, I also maintain a blog on OpenOffice.org issues, including the OpenDocument Format.

We also have blogs and other articles featured on our Newsletter on OpenOffice.org that complement these articles.
