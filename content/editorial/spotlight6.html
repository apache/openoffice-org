Spotlight: On Developers and Technology


Building Momentum.

The last ten days of April have seen unprecedented activity throughout the site. In no small part, that activity is due to the nearly simultaneous release of the 625 build, news of the Mac OS X port, and the advent of our new mirrors. Discussion in the general discuss forum has touched on a variety of subjects related to using the new build and to considerations regarding the configuration of the software. For the more technically sophisticated, the porting forum has proved lively and interesting.
