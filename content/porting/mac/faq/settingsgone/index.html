Where Has My OpenOffice.org Settings Gone?

There are a number of places that the OpenOffice.org settings have been stored on the Mac through the OpenOffice.org 2.X series. A summary of the locations is shown in the table below.

Location of OpenOffice.org settings under Mac OS X
Location First Version Last Version
~/.openoffice.org2/ 2.0.0 2.0.2
~/Library/Application Support/OpenOffice.org 2.0/ 2.0.3 2.0.4
~/Library/Application Support/OpenOffice.org 2.1/ 2.1 2.1
~/Library/Application Support/OpenOffice.org 2.2/ 2.2 2.2

When you upgrade OpenOffice.org, you can simply move your old settings to the new location. In the future, we will move to a location, where the settings will not change until OpenOffice.org 3.0.
