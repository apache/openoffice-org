Aqua Pre-Release Version

There are now development snapshots available for the Aqua version of OpenOffice.org.

THESE BUILDS SHOULD BE USED AT YOUR OWN RISK FOR TESTING PURPOSES ONLY. THEY MAY CRASH OR CAUSE DATA LOSS.

The best way to keep up with the Mac Port for Aqua is to read the blogs of the Mac Port developers. Important news will be made available in the news section.
