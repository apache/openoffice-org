3rd May 2007

Sun Officially Supports the Mac Port of OpenOffice.org

Sun are now officially supporting the Mac port of OpenOffice.org by providing 2 paid software engineers, with many years experience of OpenOffice development, to work full time on the Mac port. This is a great boost to the Mac port as we should now be able to produce the Aqua version even faster.

The Mac porting community are delighted to be working with full time Sun engineers on porting OpenOffice.org over to the Mac.
