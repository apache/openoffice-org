Donations to the Mac Porting Team

Last updated by Eric Hoch on January 20, 2005

Here is a list of the Donations:

  • April 2005: Power Mac G5 by Mr. MIYAHARA, Toru, CEO of Begi.net
  • September 2005: Apple Developer Connection select membership and Developer Transition Kit (MacTel) by Cusoon
  • October 2005: Power Mac G5 by Team OpenOffice.org e.V.. Our Tinderbox
  • November 2005: Apple Developer Connection select membership and Developer Transition Kit (MacTel) by OpenOffice.org Deutschland e.V.
  • December 2005: Three Mac Mini from Apple
  • January 2006: Intel iMac by Mr. MIYAHARA, Toru, CEO of Begi.net

The OpenOffice.org MacOSX porting team thanks all of these donators (and those who are not mentioned) for their support. These donations helped make the port of OpenOffice.org to the MacOSX X11, MacOSX Aqua (native interface) and MacTel (MacOSX on the Intel processor platform) easier.
