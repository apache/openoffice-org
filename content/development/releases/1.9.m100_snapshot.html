these notes contains changes between 680_m96 and  680_m100.
this release will install as OpenOffice.org 1.9.100.
Sources can be received from cvs by tag SRC680_m100.

Language packs for es, fr, it, sv, de, pt-BR, ja, ko, zh-CN, zh-TW are available on http://oootranslation.services.openoffice.org/pub/OpenOffice.org/680m100/ .
