OOo 2.0 (SRC680/aka 2.0.x)

The SRC680 workspaces represents the codeline for OpenOffice.org 2.0.x releases. 
  • new features for OpenOffice.org according to Product Concept Document for 2.0
  • plan and overview for 2.0
  • Bugfixes
  • Ports, Localizations

Status:

SRC680 builds are of beta quality. But it is the actual development codeline.
A Guide to New Features is available at the marketing project.

IssueZilla Bughandling:

Query for open OOo 2.0 issues.


Plan:

  • OpenOffice.org 2.0.4: September 2006
  • OpenOffice.org 2.1: December 2006
  • OpenOffice.org 2.2: March 2007
