Incubator Areas of OpenOffice.org

Incubator Contact: Louis Suárez-Potts

The Incubator category exists to provide a space for community members to test ideas. These ideas can be coding or not. Any member of Apache OpenOfficeis entitled to propose a new Incubator area. All you need is the evident support of the community. If you would like to propose a new Incubator project, please subscribe to the Apache OpenOffice Development List.
