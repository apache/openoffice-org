QA Writer - Mission Statement

The aim of the OOo QA Writer Team is to assure and improve the quality of the OpenOffice.org Writer (word processor) as well as nearby areas such as Math (formula editor), bibliography, fonts, linguistic (spell check) and printing. To achieve this, we use automated tests, testing with TCS (Test Case Specifications) and functional testing on Solaris, Linux and Windows platforms. To assure the quality of new Writer features, we participate in iTeams to have appropriate TCS ready before a feature gets integrated.
