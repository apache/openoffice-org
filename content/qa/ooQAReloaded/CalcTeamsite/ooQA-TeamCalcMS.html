QA Calc - Mission Statement

The OOo QA Calc Team has the main responsibility to assure and improve the quality of the OOo/SUN Staroffice Spreadsheet area. To this area mainly belongs: Calc core features, Calc and OASIS XML file format, spreadsheet filters, Chart and Help system. The testing happens mainly by using the office, sporadic functional testing, testing with TCS (Test Case Specification) and with automatic tests mainly on Windows, Linux and Solaris. Also new features are guided on their way into the product to assure their integration under best possible conditions.
