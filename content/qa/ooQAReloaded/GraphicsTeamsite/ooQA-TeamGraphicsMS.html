QA Graphics - Mission Statement

The OOo QA Graphics Team has the main responsibility to assure and improve the quality of the OOo/SUN StarOffice Graphics area, including Draw (Drawing) and Impress (Presentation). The testing process mainly consists of automated and manual testing on Windows, Linux and Solaris. Automated testing is done with the Testtool, manual testing by working with Test Case Specifications and functional tests. New features are guided on their way into the product to assure their integration under best possible conditions.
