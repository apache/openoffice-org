Test Case: file_bus02

Test Area: Solaris - Linux - Windows

Test Aim
Verify OpenOffice.org can create a Business Card document
Description:
    1. From the menu bar,  select File - New - Business Cards.
    2. The Business Cards dialog should be launched.
    3. Ensure there are six tab keys on the dialog showing Medium, Business Cards, Private, Business, Format and Options.
    4. Under options ensure Entire Page is selected on the Business Cards dialog.
    5. Select New Document on the Business Cards dialog.
    6. Ensure a Business Cards document is created.
    7. Close the Business Cards Document.
Expected Result:
All operations should complete successfully.

 

End of test case
