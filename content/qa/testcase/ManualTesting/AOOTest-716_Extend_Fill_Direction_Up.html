Test Case AOOTest-716: (Extend) Fill_Direction_Up [Version : 1]
Author:undefined - 19/11/2012 10:29:18
Summary:

To verify that automatically fill works properly - fill direction - up. Fills a selected range of at least two rows with the contents of the bottom most cell. If a selected range has only one column, the content of the bottom most cell is copied into the selected cells. If several columns are selected, the contents of the bottom most cells are copied into those selected above.
