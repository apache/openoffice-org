Test Case AOOTest-1769: Bookmarks-Show/Not Show [Version : 1]
Author:undefined - 21/11/2013 03:43:57
Summary:

Check OOXML(MS Word) support with feature Bookmark(Show or Not Show) , in AOO

Preconditions:

Need apache.org account to get sample document

#:Step actions:Expected Results:
1

Open the sample document MS2007FFCSW_Bookmark_Bookmark0000.docx from svn(https://svn.apache.org/repos/asf/openoffice/test/test-data/ooxml/MS_Word_2007/bookmark/) in MS Word 2007 (see attached screenshot: MS2007FFCSW_Bookmark_Bookmark0000.jpg)
 

2

Display the bookmark indicators by following setting:

  1. Click the File tab and then click Options.
  2. Click Advanced, and then select the Show bookmarks check box under Show document content.
  3. Click OK.

If you assign a bookmark to a block of text or to an item, the bookmark will appear in brackets ([…]) on the screen. If you assign a bookmark to a location, the bookmark will appear as an I-beam. The brackets and I-beam are not printed.

brackets ([…]) and I-beam are applied to the sample document

3

Open it in AOO

It can be opened successfully

4

Check the bookmarks in step 2 and step 3, to see display in step 3 is the same with display in step 2

It displays same between MS Word and AOO Writer
