Accounts you need for OOo.org QA

You only need one account to have access to a lot of great features the OpenOffice.org source project community has to offer.

Simply put, all you have to do is sign up here:

http://qa.openoffice.org/servlets/Join


Why should you sign up for an account?

First of all, it helps show the world that you are a proud user of OpenOffice.org software.

You get an <your userid>@openoffice.org email address that forwards email to another email account of your choice.

You can use your account to join the various projects that the OpenOffice.org site hosts. Hopefully you are reading this because you want to join the QA project .

Gives you basic access to IZ, so that you can post issues on bugs with OpenOffice.org or ideas for improvements and features. You also have the ability to add comments to issues that other users submit.
