2 Roundtable

2.1 Behaviour in DeactivatePage()

Currently there is a strange behaviour in DeactivatePage() in classes derived from SfxTabDialog. Not in all cases there is correct handling of NULL-pointers which might lead to a crash. Because this works at the moment, there is no need to change this immediately but kept in mind when changing calling logic of DeactivatePage() in SfxTabDialog.

2.2 Alignment of CheckBoxes

DR mentioned, that the alignment of CheckBoxes is not consistent at all. They should be indent when structured in one section. Since this is not a heavy problem, this could be done whenever modifying in entire dialogs.
