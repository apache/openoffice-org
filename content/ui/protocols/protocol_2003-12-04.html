2 Roundtable

DR announced that he created some template classes to simplify the code in the overloaded methods SfxTabPage::FillItemSet and SfxTabPage::Reset. They hold a connection between a dialog control (TriStateBox, MetricField, NumericField, ...) and an appropriate SfxPoolItem (SfxUInt16Item, SfxBoolItem....) and take care for the detection of the SfxItemState and modification of the value. He will clarify with Mathias Bauer where this templates should be located.
