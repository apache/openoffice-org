2 Roundtable

2.1 Color-Selection-Dialog

We had a little discussion about the idea to plugin the system-color-selection-dialog instead of using our own dialog, because our dialog is too confusing. But we think the system dialogs are not better. The product management will have an eye on it.



2.2 Options

It is planned in the further future to split the options into two parts. An application part and a document part. At the moment the mix of these parts confuses the users.



2.3 High Contrast

Daniel had found a bug with imagebuttons in white high contrast mode. Some colors of the images of the imagebuttons will inverted automatically. This is not correct, but only for buttons in toolbars.
