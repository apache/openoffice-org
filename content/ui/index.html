OpenOffice.org User Interface Design Project

This project focusses on technical user interface design of OpenOffice.org. Accessibility and visual design are covered as well.

Participation

Much of our work takes place in our mailing lists page, which can viewed by clicking on the "Mailing Lists" link in the navbar top left. From there, you can subscribe to our lists and view archives. Please familiarize yourself with the discussions that have taken place. Once you have done so, don't hesitate to join in! A convention is that new members introduce themselves briefly before they present ideas and opinions to the community.
