What is the difference between Add-Ons, Add-Ins, and Plugins with respect to OpenOffice.org?

  1. Add-Ons are components that integrate into the OOo GUI.
  2. Add-Ins are components that provide Calc formulas.
  3. Plugins are what you know from your browser.
