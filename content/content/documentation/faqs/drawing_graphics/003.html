How do I crop an image in my drawing?

With the image already inserted into your drawing file:

  1. Click once on the image to select it. Just below the command bar, the image toolbar will appear.
  2. In this toolbar, click on the right-most Crop button to open the Crop pop-up window.
  3. Insert the exact measurements of the canvas you want to crop.
  4. Click the OK button to close the window.
  5. Click outside the image to deselect it.
