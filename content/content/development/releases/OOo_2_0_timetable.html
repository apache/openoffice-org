    OpenOffice.org 2.0 timetable

This table describes a more detailed timetable on for OOo 2.0.
  • The green rows are describing what happened in the past, that means a description of the features implemented (described in overview in http://development.openoffice.org/releases/q-concept.html ).
  • The purple row gives an indication of the current status (where we are now).
  • The yellow rows are the plan for the future. The dates give an indication when this feature will be implemented by development, there will be a gap between this date and actual reintegration build for the time needed for QA approval and reintegration of the master.
  • The red rows gives an indication where we are behind our original plan.
