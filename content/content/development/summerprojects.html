OpenOffice.org Summer of Code Projects

This last summer (in the Northern Hemisphere), OpenOffice.org participated in the Summer of Code (SoC) initiative sponsored by Google. The SoC projects are listed below. Some of the participant students also graciously agreed to be interviewed, and their interviews can be linked from their names below. From their accounts, it is fair to say that the Summer of Code was a smashing success! Thanks to Google for making it possible and thanks especially to the students and mentors for making this last summer a great summer.
