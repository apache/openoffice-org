2. Replaceable context menus

The design for the interface for replaceable context menus should be discussed in the public. We agreed that it should contain the selected object(s) and optionally the mouse position. The mouse position only does not make sense, because context menus can be opened without mouse. It could be that there even does not exist a mouse.

There is no decision yet on including this feature in the Final6.0. Problems are the expected effort of time and the risk of damaging anything else near completion of the Final.
