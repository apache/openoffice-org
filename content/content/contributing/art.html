Participate in OpenOffice.org

Graphics and Art

Have any art skills? Then you can help us create icons, logos, banners, labels, and more! These will be seen every day and used throughout the project and its products.

If you are interested, consider joining our new Art Project. The Art Project is working closely with other open source projects, and is the center of art activity for the OpenOffice.org world.

Read the provisions of the Art Project for donating images there. You might also find it useful to visit our Guidelines for Participate in OpenOffice.org page, which outlines the way in which your work can be channeled into this project.
