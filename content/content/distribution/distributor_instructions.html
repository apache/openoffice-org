Distributor Instructions

Thank you for your interest in distributing Apache OpenOffice.

If you haven't already done so, please start by reading our main Distribution FAQ page. Please contact private@openoffice.apache.org with the following information about your distribution:

  1. Company name
  2. Company Location
  3. Company contact information (name, e-mail address)
  4. Company website link which provides additional information including cost.
  5. Product/Distribution name
  6. Additional information on the distribution: packaged download directly from SourceForge, your own customized package directly from source, specific OS platforms, etc.
