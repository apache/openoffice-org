CD-ROMs for consumers

Note: OpenOffice.org does not supply CD ROMs directly. If you want a CD, you need to follow the directions below to find a distributor who services your area.

This page allows you to find a distributor near you, or who will ship to your location.

Please note: Distributors charge a small fee to cover their costs and postage. Once you have contacted them, please ensure that you check your email regularly for their reply. You must do this for at least the next 24 hours. Until you have agreed to their price, they will not ship the CDs.

There is also a set of 3 standard CD images available for you to burn onto your own CD-R. To download these images,

see the CD Download page
