Downloading the OpenOffice.org software

De {xx}localization fo OpenOffice.org (build 641D) for Windows or Linux you can download at below sites:

  • http://some-mirror/openoffice/} ({country})
  • http://some-other-mirror/openoffice} ({other country})
  • etc.

To get the English builds and the sources, you can also look to the above sites, and also at www.openoffice.org itself: http://www.openoffice.org/dev_docs/source/latest_build/latest_build.html

Apart from the "stable build" 1.0 there is also a build 642; it is a so called "developers build". It offers a preview to further developments of OpenOffice.org, buit would be less suitable for daily use. If you are interested to evaluate build 642, then you could do this with the English version, see the above mentioned download page of www.openoffice.org

Note: the files for the spell checking must be separately downloaded.
