Marketing Communications and Information Contacts

The Marketing Team can help you with introducing OpenOffice in your organization, arrange for speakers at meetings, or present detailed case studies of other OpenOffice implementations. If you want to write a story about OpenOffice they should be your first port of call.

For further information, see What do MarCons do?. For more information, or if you want to get involved, contact the Marketing Team on the marketing mailing list.
