Traveling Guide - From Airport to Beijing Friendship Hotel

The Beijing Capital International Airport (PEK) is about 30 kilometers away from the Beijing Friendship Hotel. You can use several kinds of public transportation from there to the hotel.


1. Taxi

At the exit of the arrival hall, you can take a taxi to the hotel. Usually, the expense for the trip is about 90 Yuan RMB. (It will cost more if there is traffic jam or at night.) Additionally, you have to reimburse a road fee of 10 Yuan RMB to the taxi driver. If you don't speak Chinese, you can print out this sheet and show it to the taxi driver, we recommend this.


Please point the taxi driver on the address of your hotel below. He might also call the hotels number for more information.




Beijing Friendship Hotel: (86) 010 68498888,

Tips: You can take a taxi from any place in the city back to the hotel. Remember to take the cab with an authorized sign of “Taxi”, pay the fee shown on the taximeter inside the cab and keep the printed receipt.
