Mission statement:
To create and maintain a central repository of OpenOffice.org related graphics and artwork, and to provide dedicated tools and a proper environment for graphical art creation and internationalization in the benefit of the OpenOffice.org Community and Projects.

Galleries:
These galleries include artwork for different purposes:
The official part of a gallery contains artwork that should be used for referring to OpenOffice.org as product or as community. To maintain our visual identity, please use this artwork wherever possible. The previous artwork refers to OpenOffice.org 1.x versions. We don't want to remove this artwork from the galleries, so if you need older artwork, just have a look here. Additional artwork can be utilized for private usage or as basis for your own artwork. However we strongly recommend that the official artwork be used for public display.
