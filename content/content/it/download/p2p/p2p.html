(Back to P2P project)
This page contains a list of all the versions of OpenOffice.org that are available via P2P.  The majority of the downloads are through BitTorrent.  There are also downloads available through the ed2k network.

A P2P client needs to be installed for downloading.  The following are examples of clients:

If the link does not automatically download when you click it, your client is not configured to start with the P2P automatically. Copy the link into your client to allow it to start downloading.

You can also find our BitTorrent downloads at our main tracker at http://borft.student.utwente.nl:6969/
How to download with P2P:

(1) Download and install an appropriate P2P client

(2) Find the version of OpenOffice that you wish to download below

(3) Once the file has downloaded please keep your client open to allow your computer to share the file
