Currently hosted are the following files:

office-spec.sxw

Working document for the OASIS Open Office TC specification. This draft is only used internally by the TC.

Note: The current committee draft is available here.

extract.xsl

XSLT to extract the Relax NG from the specification document.

May also be used to extract text of a specific format from any OASIS Open Office or OpenOffice.org document.

oasis2ooo.xsl

XSLT to transform OASIS Open Office into OpenOffice.org documents.

ooo2oasis.xsl

XSLT to transform OpenOffice.org into OASIS Open Office documents.

CVS history and past versions of these documents may be accessed through anonymous CVS or a CVS web front end. The CVS path to the documents is xml/www/oasis.
