Initial setup (Chatzilla)

(1) After installing the Chatzilla plugin, you will need to restart your browser.

(2) On Firefox, Chatzilla is installed under the Toos menu. Go to Tools > Chatzilla to start.

(3) Your initial screen should look similar to this one.

(4) There is only one configuration step. You need to choose an IRC nickname. Go to Tools > Options. Click on the General tab and type in a Nickname.

Note: A Nickname can only consist of letters, numbers and _under_scores_. No spaces allowed.

Note: If your Nickname is already taken, you will have to come back here and pick a new one.
