Bi-weekly IRC lightning talks

Conferences are held at FreeNode (irc.freenode.net) , on the channel #ooonlc. We have instructions to help you get started with IRC. You can see coming talks and previous talks.

About the IRC talks

Setup The talks are short (~20min) and informal. They are followed by a Question & Answer period, and then group discussion.
Purpose To help different language communities share ideas, and meet in a friendly environment.
Help! If you wish to give a talk, please contact Daniel Carrera (dcarrera@openoffice.org).
