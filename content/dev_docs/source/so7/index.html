You can download the ProductUpdate 5 from http://sunsolve.sun.com/pub-cgi/show.pl?target=patches/xprod-StarOffice&nav=pub-patches. ProductUpdate 5 includes all fixes from ProductUpdate 1, 2, 3 and 4.

This patch also fixes the most frequently occurring crash-related bugs that were sent in by the StarOffice 7 and OpenOffice.org1.1 Stack-Reporter.
