System Requirements for OpenOffice.org 1.0.x

Solaris Operating Environment (SPARC Platform Edition):

  • 240-MBytes hard disk space Solaris 7 or 8 Operating Environment (SPARC Platform Edition)
  • Solaris 8, requires patches 108434-01 and 108435-01 (64-bit)
  • Solaris 7, requires patches 106327-08, 106300-09 (64-bit), and 106327-07
  • For the Asian version, users must use the Solaris 8 platform, with patches 108434-01, 108435-01 (64-bit), and 108773-07
  • For patches go to: http://sunsolve.sun.com
  • CDE 1.3 or higher (recommended) or OpenWindows[tm]
  • 64-MBytes RAM minimum, 128-Mbytes RAM recommended
  • VGA or higher resolution graphic device with at least 256 colors, 800 x 600

 

GNU/Linux ("Linux"):

  • Pentium-compatible PC or higher
  • 64-MBytes RAM recommended
  • 250-Mbytes hard disk space
  • Linux Kernel 2.2.13 or higher
  • X Server with 800x600 or higher resolution and a minimum of 256 colors or grayscale
  • Installed glibc2 version number 2.1.3 or higher
  • VGA or higher resolution graphic device with at least 256 colors, 800 x 600

 

Microsoft Windows:

  • Pentium-compatible PC
  • Microsoft Windows 95, 98, NT, 2000 or XP (2000, ME or XP required for Asian/CJK versions)
  • 64-Mbytes RAM minimum
  • 250-Mbytes hard disk space
