Posting Rates for OOo Mailing Lists on gmane.org

part of Information Sharing project

55 of OpenOffice.org's mailing lists are piped to the newsgroup service gmane.org, which in turn provides statistics for each list. The charts are grouped by category and roughly sorted by the maximum of messages/day during the period. Note that the names do not match the alias names on OOo because gmane.org has it's own scheme.
