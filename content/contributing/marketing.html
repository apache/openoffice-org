Participate in OpenOffice.org

Marketing

You can always help promote the use of OpenOffice.org. Here are two ways you can help:

  • Join the marketing project
    The aims of the marketing project are to further the growth and use of OpenOffice.org throughout the world and act as a communications group within the project. To learn more about this opportunity, visit the Marketing project home page.

  • Distribute OpenOffice.org
    We need people to distribute OpenOffice.org CDs or set up download mirrors. Whether you give them away for free, or sell them at a profit, you will be helping OpenOffice.org reach more users. If you visit the Distribution project home page you will learn about distributing OpenOffice.org and how we can help.
