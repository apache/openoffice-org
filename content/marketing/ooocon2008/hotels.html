Accomodation

The organizers will take care of the hotel bookings centrally. This will ensure dramatically discounted prices. As a guide, rooms will be available with prices ranging from Euro 20 per night in 3-star hotel (double room) up to Euro 120 per night in 5-star hotel (single room). As a positive side effect, networking will be easier!

Lunch will be provided free during the conference days. On the first day, the organizers would like to invite all attendees to a welcome dinner.
