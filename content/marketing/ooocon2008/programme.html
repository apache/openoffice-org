Conference Programme

The OpenOffice.org Conference 2008 will extend over three days, Wednesday 5th November to Friday 7th November inclusive. The Conference will open on 9am on Wednesday, and close on Friday 5.00pm.

Details of these three days may be found here:

  • Wednesday 5th.
  • Thursday 6th.
  • Friday 7th.

The Conference organizers are also planning two days of free tourist trips (Great Wall, Forbidden City, etc) over the weekend of 8th-9th November.

In addition to these public events, facilities will be provided for individual projects to hold events for their members on Tuesday 4th.

In parallel, the ODF Adoption TC will be holding a a full-day workshop on Thursday.
