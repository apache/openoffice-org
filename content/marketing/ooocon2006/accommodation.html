Accommodation

Please, don't hesitate to ask on the mailing list for more informations or for any help you may need to book your room. Along this page you will find some icons, here is their meaning:

  • link to the place website
  • view the position on a map

INSA Campus

Some rooms will be available on the INSA campus. The price is around 25 € per night. The exact number and location of the rooms is not known for the moment, but you will be able to ask for a room during the registration. Note that there won't be enought rooms for everybody.

Hotel

  • Tourism Office is providing a list of hotel available in Lyon. Click on English version link.
  • Accor Group has rooms ranging from  41€ to 500€. On line reservations are available.
  • Lyon city site offers also an hotel list where you can make on line reservation
