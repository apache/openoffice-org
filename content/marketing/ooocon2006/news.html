News

Sep. 11, 2006 NeoOffice News: Aqua is here! NeoOffice.org is pleased to announce the immediate availability of NeoOffice 2.0 Aqua Beta 3. This native open source port of OpenOffice.org to Mac OS X now features a complete Aqua user interface including native file open and save dialogs, and all of the great Mac OS X specific features hundreds of thousands of users have grown to love. Download it from http://www.neooffice.org or stop by our booth at OOoCon 2006 and pick up your free copy today!

Sep. 12, 2006 NeoOffice News: We are pleased to announce a partnership with Conjour to provide full 24/7 commercial support for NeoOffice for companies, NGOs, and other organizations. Continue checking http://www.neooffice.org for information on availability.
