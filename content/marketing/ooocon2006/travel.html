Travel informations

Better prices for the travel

As Air France is our official carrier, you can get savings for your flight between Sept 8, 2006 and Sept 16, 2006. You will have to put you name and surname on this document and print it. You have to print the document and take it with you during your reservation and travel.

You can save up to 45% for flights in metropolitan france. For international flights, there might be savings too, but we do not have any rate. For any problems with this agreement, contact Air France.

The place

The OpenOffice.org Conference 2006 will take place at INSA Lyon located 20 Avenue Albert Einstein, on the DOUA Campus of Lyon (France).

Coming to Lyon

By plane

The Lyon Saint Exupery Airport  will be your arrival Airport. See the WorldAirportGuide for informations about this airport

Once at the airport, take the airport shuttle for downtown Lyon ("Centre de Lyon") (Lyon Part-Dieu train station) which runs every 20 min. For further information (itineraries, timetable, prices) visit the airport bus line site at: http://www.satobus.com/welcome.html. The shuttle will bring you to the Lyon Part-Dieu train station.

By car

EU citizens use their regular license. Everyone else (with some exceptions) should obtain an international drivers license. You can come to INSA by Road:

  • Via "Rocade Est" ring road: exit 1B then "Croix Luizet", follow "la Doua", then "Domaine Scientifique de la Doua".
  • Via the Boulevard Laurent BONNEVAY: exit 6 "Porte de Croix Luizet", then follow direction "Campus de la Doua".

You can prepare your route with those sites :

  • http://www.rhonealpes-tourisme.com/
  • http://www.viamichelin.co.uk/

Speed limits in France:

  • 130 km/h (~85 mph) on highways
  • 110 km/h (~65 mph) on roads reserved for motor vehicles
  • 90 km/h (~60 mph) on other roads
  • 50 km/h in towns

By train

Two railway station offer an access to Lyon: Lyon Part-Dieu and Lyon Perrache. The SNCF site will help you to book your tickets to one of these station.

Coming to INSA

Once at Lyon Part-Dieu or Lyon Perrache train station, take the tramway T1 direction "IUT Feyssine" and get off at "Croix-Luizet station"
