Closing messages from the Workshop:

Remember - technical excellence is not enough.

If OpenOffice.org is to become the leading international office suite, it needs good marketing.

Community members have a wonderful message, but it needs to be written down and communicated. Having a good Strategic Marketing Plan is not an option for the Community - it is essential.
