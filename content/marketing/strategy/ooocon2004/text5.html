Features and Benefits

  • Features: "things" we build into OOo or provide as a Community

  • Benefits: the value this brings to the user

  • For example:

    • Feature: Open-source licence
    • Benefit: you can pass copies on to friends, family, students, employees... (legitimately!)

  • Users do not choose OOo for its features...

  • ...they use it for the benefits it brings


Notes:

Features and Benefits is a key marketing concept.

In the Community we are often pre-occupied with product features - will this particular 'feature' make its way into the next release.

From a user's perspective, a 'feature' has to pass the 'so what' test before it brings them any benefit:

Feature: OpenOffice.org has an open-source licence
So What?
Benefit: you can give it away to other people
