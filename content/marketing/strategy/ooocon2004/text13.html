Brainstorm 3

  • Next 5 years
  • What will be the major influences on the market for office suites? (Social, Technological, Economic, Political / Legal)
  • Are these Opportunities or Threats to OOo?

    Notes:

    Participants were invited to give their forecasts for significant trends in the wider world that could influence the market for office suites.

    Trends could be opportunities, or threats, or in some cases both!
