OpenOffice.org Conference (OOoCon 2005)
Press Conference Registration Form

September, 29th at 12.00.
Koper/Capodistria, Pretorian Palace, Tito's square 1

The third OpenOffice.org Conference will be kept in Koper-Capodistria from the 28 to the 30 september, organized from the communities of the Italian and Slovenian OpenOffice.org linguistic projects , under the auspices of the Region Friuli Venezia Giulia, the province of Trieste, the Municipality of Trieste, the Slovenian Ministry for the High Education, Science and Technology, the Municipality of Koper-Capodistria, and with the collaboration of the Linux User Group Trieste, the Linux User Group Slovenia, the Italian Union and the Community of the Italians of Capodistria Santorio-Santorio, the Theatre of Capodistria, Primorska University and Faculty of Pedagogy, Cyberpipe and Youthful and Culture Center of Koper-Capodistria.

The Press conference will be an occasion for discussing about the free software situation in Europe along with the state of the OpenOffice.org suite, in a moment in which there are always more companies and agencies that they announce of wanting to abandon the proprietary solutions in favor of free software solutions.

Partecipants:

  • Welcome of President of Italian Union in Koper-Capodistria
  • Senator Fiorello Cortiana, italian representative at WSIS (World Summit of Information Society).
  • Gianni Pecol Cominotto, Councillor responsible for Organization, Human Resources and Information Technology for the Region Friuli Venezia Giulia
  • Joze Zrimsek, general secretar of Directorate for Information Society of Slovenia
  • Robert Ludvik, Urska Colner and Davide Dozza maintainer of Slovenian and Italian language projects and conference organizers
  • Louis Suarez Potts, representative of OpenOffice.org Community Council
  • Tim Bray, Director of Web Technologies at Sun Microsystems and co-chair of the IETF AtomPub Working Group
  • Micheal Meeks, OpenOffice.org Novell developer

Journalists and interested people are invited to register by compiling the form above.
