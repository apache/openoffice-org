Location (country, city)

Latvia, Riga

Latvia lies between Estonia to the north, Russia and Belarus to the east, the Baltic Sea to the west and Lithuania to the south. It is the ʻcentralʼ Baltic country in both size, business activity, communications and geographical location.

Riga is emerging as a top congress and conference city in Europe thanks to its strategic location, its rich and exiting culture, its unspoiled nature and its easy access by air from around Europe and around the globe.

Latvia has a long history of technological innovation with highlights including the Minox spy camera and one of Europe's largest pre-war telephone networks. Today, Latvia continues this tradition with original scientific research and by hosting a number of companies that use open source software in embedded and professional solutions. Latvia has an astonishing transition, going from s Soviet republic to a member of the EU and the NATO alliance.

Section 2 – Proposal

In 400 words or less, answer this question:

Why should the Community award OOoCon 2009/2010 to your team?

The opportunity to organize OOoCon2009 in Riga is an opportunity to broaden ODF and OpenOffice.org popularity and recognition in Latvia, Eastern Europe and around the globe.

  • Free and Open Source Software (FOSS) in Latvia is currently:
    • In limited use by government
    • Not widely adopted by education institutions and enterprises
    • Facing resistance to change from a user base that is accustomed to pirated solutions, making for a ripe environment for raising OpenOffice.org and FOSS awareness people got used to existing usually pirated solutions
  • LATA would drive promotions OO.o and FOSS through OooCon2009, boosting:
    • Recognition of OO.o and FOSS by those who are unfamiliar with it
    • Adoption of OO.o and FOSS by those who have heard about it
    • Promotion and distribution of OO.o and FOSS by those who have tried it
    • Contribution to the OO.o and FOSS community and projects by regional experts

This year offers a special opportunity to promote open source in Latvia and the region as we face our most difficult economic crisis since regaining our independence. Our public and private sector institutions face unprecedented budgetary challenges and would benefit greatly from adopting open source software, especially under the free-download/enterprise support model offered by Sun.

As a venue, Riga offers and unparalleled combination: truly original and preserved architecture and a wealth of experiences within walking distance. Riga's Old town and beautiful city centre is compact, romantic and full of fun opportunities.

Riga benefits:

  • A unique, economical environment for small to mid-sized conferences
  • Central access to the Baltic market of 7.5 million people
  • A Norhtern crossroads where East-West truly meets
  • Attractive prices for goods and services
  • Attractive cafés, bars gourmet restaurants
  • The hottest night life in Northern Europe

Our Riga OOoCon 2009 team have already laid the groundwork for this event: the LATA conference 2008 was a major success with nearly 300 attendees and 16 speakers from around the world. We also organized Software Freedom Day 2008 in partnership with the Sun/MySQL team during their world wide developers conference in Riga, during which the team was able to benefit from Riga's wide selection of culinary options, entertainment and health services.

Let OOoCon 2009 in Riga serve as a strong base for the future growth of OO.o and FOSS recognition and adoption in Latvia, Baltics and all of Eastern Europe!
