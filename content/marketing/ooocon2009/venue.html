Conference Venue

Meeting Facilities

The meeting venue is The Palazzo del Popolo Conference Centre and the Palazzo dei Sette. They are both located in the heart of the city. They are all equipped with wireless-lan (for each participant), amplification systems, audiovisual systems for projection and recording, teleconferencing systems. Every speech will be recorded in audio and video and a videostreaming service for the OOoCon will be setup. Several rooms will be available for public and private group meetings.
