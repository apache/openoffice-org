Description of SVG files

The SVG files can be looked at with some browsers (like Konqueror) and worked on with vector graphic programs like Inkscape. This allows you to export them in different sizes and formats (please make sure that the small wire gull lines will not be broadened during the export).

They include the different subtitles ("Community Distributor" and "Extras") in specific layers.
These layers are hidden by default, but they can be showed and included in the printout, if you want to.

The different vector graphic programs use specific functions to show hidden layers, in Inkscape you'll have to select the layer (called "Distributor" or "Extras") from the pop up list in the middle of the status line and to click on the closed eye symbol directly left to this list.
