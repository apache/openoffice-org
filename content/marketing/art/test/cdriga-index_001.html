Mission statement:
To create and maintain a central repository of OpenOffice.org related graphics and artwork, to provide a proper environment that encourages the graphical art creation and participation of various artists and to assist in internationalization and translation of the graphics by cooperating with the native language projects in an attempt to provide a coordinated and unified graphical identity for the OpenOffice.org project.

Areas covered by the project include (but are not limited to) posters, screenshots, CD-covers, OpenOffice.org related wallpapers and other promotional graphics, GUI graphics, web graphics, labels, logos, etc. All the above will be created and maintained in tight coordination with the respective projects that need the graphics.
