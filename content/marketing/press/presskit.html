OpenOffice.org Press Kit

Last updated May 7, 2002


News!

We have just released OpenOffice.org 638c for Mac OS X (Developer Build). To learn more of this exciting release, please read our General FAQ.

Welcome to the Press Kit page! Here are all the materials needed to easily familiarize members of the press, new users and developers from all over the world with OpenOffice.org, the project, and OpenOffice.org 1.0, the product.

The marketing materials featured on this page are freely available to the outside publishing community for their own use, and to OpenOffice.org users and developers for building physical press kits that will be distributed at conferences and presentations.
