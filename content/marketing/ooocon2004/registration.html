Registration

The admission fee will be EUR 20. The fee will be collected at the event and includes the admission, cold beverages and an OpenOffice.org CD.

Send your informal registration via email to
confregistration@openoffice.org

Your registration email should include the following information:

  • Full Name
  • Email Address
  • Company/Affiliation (if any)
  • Country
  • Area of Interest (General and/or Development)

Registration is open Wednesday 14:00 - 18:00 and Thursday starting at 8:00.

For attendees we have a mailing list: ooocon2004_discuss@marketing.openoffice.org
If you would like to chat with other attendees, subscribe by sending an empty message to
ooocon2004_discuss-subscribe@marketing.openoffice.org

 

... and stay tuned for further information...
