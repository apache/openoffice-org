Location

We are glad to host the Conference in Berlin, Germany.
The Humboldt-Universität generously offered the facilities to host the conference. The OpenOffice.org Conference will take place in Erwin Schrödinger-Zentrum.

Erwin Schrödinger-Zentrum Adlershof
Rudower Chaussee 26
12489 Berlin
Germany

Accomodations

With the help of Berlin Tourismus Information we are able to provide a list of hotels. Check out the overview map to see where the hotels are located.

 

For conference attendees we have a mailing list: ooocon2004_discuss@marketing.openoffice.org
If you would like to chat with other attendees, subscribe by sending an empty message to
ooocon2004_discuss-subscribe@marketing.openoffice.org
