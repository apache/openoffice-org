Mission statement:

Our goal is to reveal positive associations with the brand OpenOffice.org by improving identification of the community members with OpenOffice.org and recognition of the product in public.

Therefore we're creating and maintaining a consistent virtual design with repetitive use of graphical elements and artwork for all appearances of our brand.

As this work is related not only to marketing, but also to several other OpenOffice.org projects, we're keeping continuous contact with website, documentation, user experience, native-language and other projects.
