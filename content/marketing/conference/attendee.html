Attendee

For registering as an attendee please go to http://marketing.openoffice.org/conference/registration.html

The admission fee will be EUR 20 and will be collected at the event. The fee includes the admission, beverages and an OpenOffice.org CD. Lunch vouchers (7,50EUR) valid in three University cafeterias are purchasable during registration.

You can make yourself familiar with the floor map for the conference area at Philosphenturm.

Welcome and Keynotes start Thurday 9:00 on time. Please note that Registration opens 8:15. If you are in Hamburg on Wednesday already you could help us to avoid long waiting queues on Thursday.
Registration is open Wednesday, 16:00 to 18:00.

Restricted network access is possible in the conference areas.
