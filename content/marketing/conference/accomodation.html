Location

We are glad to host the Conference in Hamburg, Germany.
The University of Hamburg generously offered the facilities to host the conference. The OpenOffice.org Conference will take place in Philosophenturm. There are several lecture halls (up to 350 people) and small seminar rooms available.

Travel

From/To Hamburg Airport go flights

  • hourly to Frankfurt or Munich, Germany
  • 5-8 times a day to major European Airports
  • no intercontinental flights

For flight schedules see the Timetable. A taxi from the airport to University of Hamburg costs ~20 EUR and the trip takes about 45 minutes (depending on traffic density).

For driving directions use your favorite online service like Yahoo, MapBlast or Marco Polo (German).

If you would like to go by train, the german railway Deutsche Bahn offers an online travel service.
