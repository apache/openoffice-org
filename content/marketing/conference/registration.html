Registration

The admission fee will be EUR 20 (~ USD 20) and will be collected at the event. The fee includes the admission, beverages and an OpenOffice.org CD.

Send your informal registration via email to
confregistration@openoffice.org

Your registration email has to include the following information:

  • Full Name
  • Email Address
  • Company/Affiliation (if any)
  • Country
  • Area of Interest (General and/or Development)

For attendees we have a mailing list: ooocon2003_discuss@marketing.openoffice.org
If you would like to chat with other attendees, subscribe by sending an empty message to
ooocon2003_discuss-subscribe@marketing.openoffice.org

... and stay tuned for further information...
