First Impressions from OOoCon 2003

"My sincere thanks for all the great work you put in to make OOoCon happen. It was an amazing opportunity to meet and get to know the people that are normally concealed, that do the real work. Also excellent to flesh-out those E-mail addresses into real, fascinating people.
The organisation was excellent, the speakers insightful, the attendees interesting and the atmosphere stimulating and encouraging. I think we've got great things to look forward in OO.o in the weeks and months ahead.
Thanks again, Michael."

The presentation slides are available from the schedule.

Have a look at the photos taken by Takashi Nakamoto, Stephan Schäfer, Simon Brouwer, Cristian Driga, Martin Hauge. Cristian also created some video clips.

Please send your feedback and links to photos/videos/audio to conference@marketing.openoffice.org.

We are not only fishing for compliments. During the closing Townhall Meeting topics for improvement were mentioned.

See you next year at OOoCon 2004
