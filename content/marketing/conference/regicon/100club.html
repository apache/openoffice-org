OOoRegiCon 100 Club

OOoRegiCon 100 Club

Last Updated: 9 February 2005

The 100 Club was created to recognise supporters of OOoRegiCon.

OOoRegiCon is the inaugural OpenOffice.org Regional Conference North America. It will be held 9 February 2005, Del Mar Fairgrounds in San Diego, as part of the Desktop Linux Summit. OpenOffice.org has also been invited to participate in the DLS exhibition with up to two booths.

OpenOffice.org CDROMs are required to give out to attendees (an expected audience of 1000) and funds are needed to subsidise our volunteers to travel and be accommodated for the event.

How To Join the 100 Club

I would like to extend an invitation for you to join the OOoRegiCon 100 Club.

To join the 100 Club, please donate 100 OpenOffice.org CDROMs and or $100USD.

100 CDROMS:
Please contact me Jacqueline McNally and I will provide you with an address to ship the CDROMs.

$100USD (EUR 75):
To donate money, please visit the Contributing Funds to OpenOffice.org page and indicate in the comments field that the donation is for OOoRegiCon.

Donors will be listed on this page (OOoRegiCon 100 Club) in order of receipt. Also, at OOoRegiCon and during the DLS Exhibition we will be pleased to recognise the "100 Club" as part of a poster display. Note: Please let us know if you do not wish to be listed.

All donations that are not utilised by this event will be returned to the OpenOffice.org pool to support other conferences where OpenOffice.org is represented.

Please do not hesitate to contact me if you require any further information. Do support our volunteers that are contributing to make OOoRegiCon and the OpenOffice.org exhibition a successful event.
