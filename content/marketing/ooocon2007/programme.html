Programme

Conference Dates The OpenOffice.org Conference 2007 will extend over three days, Wednesday 19th September to Friday 21st September inclusive. The Conference will open on 9am on Wednesday, and close on Friday 4.00pm

Projects Day In addition to these public events, facilities will be provided for individual projects to hold events for their members on Tuesday 18th.

Details of each day's proceedings may be found here:

  • Tuesday (project events)
  • Wednesday (Public Conference)
  • Thursday (Public Conference)
  • Friday (Public Conference)

A downloadable Conference Programme is available (30 pages - 300kb pdf).

We are also holding in parallel a one-day "ODF Camp," a full-day workshop focused on ODF interoperability among implementations. If you are interested, visit our ODF Camp page.

Other dates

11th of September is the National Day of Catalonia - many places in the city are closed.
19th - 22nd September: Drupalcon Barcelona 2007
24th September is a bank holiday in Barcelona. There is a very extensive programme of cultural activities for the day.
