How to add the OOoCon2007 template into your copy of OpenOffice.org

  • Download the template OOoCon2007.otp from the Conference site and store it on your computer.

  • Click on File -> New -> Templates and Documents to open the “Templates and Documents” panel

  • Click on “Organise...” to access the “Template Management” panel.
    Right click on “My Templates” and select “Import Template...”

  • Navigate to where you have saved OOoCon2007.otp and press “Open”.

  • “OOoCon2007” will now be visible within “My Templates”

  • Press “Close” to close the “Template Management” panel.

  • Press “Cancel” to close the “Templates and Documents” panel.


When you want to create a presentation based on this template:

  • Click on File -> New -> Templates and Documents to open the “Templates and Documents” panel

  • Click on the Templates icon on the Templates and Documents panel

  • Find “OOoCon2007” within “My Templates” and press “Open”.
