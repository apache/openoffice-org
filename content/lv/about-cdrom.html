OpenOffice.org software on CD-ROM

{this page is intended to provide links to companies or individuals who offer to sell CD-ROMs for those who don't have a fast and cheap internet connection to download the software. At this moment, it just gives information about this and calls for people willing to sell the CD-ROMs}

About offering the OpenOffice.org code on CD-ROM, see the pertinent FAQ. As the spell checker files are covered by the GPL licence, it is also permitted (and recommended) to distribute them. Please make sure the readme files are also on the CD.

A CD-ROM with OpenOffice.org should, for example, contain the following:

  • the most recent {xx}language builds for MS-Windows(x86) en voor Linux(x86)
  • the English builds for Windows(x86) en voor Linux(x86)
  • the sources
  • de tekst van de GPL, de LGPL en de SISSL (!)
  • de files for spell checking, including the corresponding readme files

When somebody wants to distribute the {xx} language builds on CD-ROM, send me the relevant information, to be listed on this page.

S.B.
