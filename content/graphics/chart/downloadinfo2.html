Testable areas in the Beta developer snapshot of the chart reimplementation  (Milestone 9)

The chart reimplementation is still ongoing. In the following you find an overview of working and non working areas to give you an idea of what you can expect from this developer snapshot. Please help with testing thus we can assure to not introduce severe regression when replacing the old chart.

Please don't test this areas as they are still under construction:

  • Drag and Drop of Data to chart

  • Copy and Paste of whole chart ( 64497)

  • Chart updates after data changes in Calc or Writer

  • Help Content

  • Performance
