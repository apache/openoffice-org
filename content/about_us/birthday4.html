OpenOffice.org Is Four

2004-10-13

Today we are four. On 13 October 2000 Sun Microsystems donated the source to StarOffice to the open-source community. Sun has continued to sponsor the project, which CollabNet hosts and manages. Our celebrations started early, in September, with the Second OOoCon, in Berlin. The papers presented there highlight the activity and promise of the project and the code.
