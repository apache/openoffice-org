About Anthony Long

Anthony Long spends most of his time working in the e-commerce department of a major cable television network, building a startup business, and helping with the marketing efforts of OpenOffice.org.

Anthony began working on OpenOffice.org while he was a full-time student at Harvard University, where he received a bachelor's degree in Sociology in June 2003. Anthony's past experience includes interning in marketing at Procter & Gamble and serving as Business Manager of a nationally distributed student-produced newsmagazine.
